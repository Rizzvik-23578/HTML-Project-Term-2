The Founding of YouTube | 2005

YouTube was founded in February 2005 by three early PayPal employees – Chad Hurley, Steve Chen and Jawed Karim. The founders created YouTube with the goal of providing a convenient platform for sharing user-generated video content on the web. The domain "YouTube.com" was activated on February 14, 2005 – just in time for Valentine‘s Day as the founders were hoping to catch eyes with their new venture. According to Jawed Karim, the inspiration for YouTube‘s name came from the phrase "Tune in to You" along with the popularity of yourtube.com for tube-shaped TV screens at the time..
